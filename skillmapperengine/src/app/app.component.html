NIIT SkillMapper provides a crystal ball that provides a platform that shows how smoothely it can be utilised.

Skill Mapper is built for Talent Acquisition and Management teams facing the challenge of building workforce and succession plans for their employees' skill sets in their current systems.

  • Employees can register and update his profile and certifications done
  • Employeer can search by respective skill requirements and can contact them
  • HR can review the current status of employee and release him for his new tasks and will be able to coordinate with the Employeer
